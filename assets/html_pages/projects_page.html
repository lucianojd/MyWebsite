Travel

My high-school hosted a grade 9 trip to Europe. I got to tour through Belgium, France, Germany, Spain, and Italy. Italy was my favourite. While in Italy I got to take a gondola ride in Venice, walk inside the dome of the Duomo in Florence, and hike along the Cinqua Terre in Liguria. In France we got to go up the Eiffel Tower. My favourite part of the trip was the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona, Spain. I've also been to Edmonton and Calgary for various volleyball tournaments.
